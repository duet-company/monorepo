# Duet Company - Vision & Mission

## Our Mission

To democratize data infrastructure by making enterprise-grade analytics
accessible to everyone through AI-driven automation.

## Our Vision

A world where any company, regardless of size or technical expertise, can
harness the power of their data without needing a dedicated data engineering
team.

## Company Values

### 1. AI-First

We believe AI should be the foundation, not an add-on. Our platform is designed
from the ground up to leverage AI for autonomous operation.

### 2. Speed to Value

Customers should see value in hours, not months. We optimize every aspect of our
platform for rapid deployment and immediate insights.

### 3. Open & Transparent

We build on open source technologies and believe in transparent operations. No
vendor lock-in, no hidden complexity.

### 4. Customer Success

Our success is measured by our customers' success. We obsess over user
experience and customer outcomes.

## The Problem We Solve

### Current State

Building a modern data platform today requires:

- **Specialized expertise** - Data engineers, DevOps, DBAs
- **Significant investment** - $100K+ in infrastructure costs
- **Long timelines** - 6-18 months to production
- **Ongoing maintenance** - Constant tuning and optimization

### Small Businesses Are Left Behind

- 95% of businesses lack dedicated data teams
- Most operate in the dark without data-driven insights
- Existing solutions are either too simple or too complex

## Our Solution

### Autonomous Data Infrastructure

Duet Company uses AI to:

- **Design** platforms from natural language requirements
- **Deploy** infrastructure automatically
- **Optimize** queries and storage continuously
- **Monitor** and self-heal 24/7
